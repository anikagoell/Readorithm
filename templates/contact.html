Frequently Asked Questions

How does Readorithm work?

Readorithm uses advanced algorithms to analyze user reading preferences and recommend books that match the taste. You can search for books, view top recommendations and get personalized suggestions based on your data analysis.

Is Readorithm free to use?

Yes, Readorithm is completely free to use. We believe in making book recommendations accessible to everyone.

How are ratings calculated?

Ratings are calculated based on a combination of user reviews, book popularity, and statistical analysis to provide an accurate representation of a book's quality and appeal.

Can I suggest a book to be added to Readorithm?

Absolutely. We welcome book suggestions from users. Please email us with the book title, author, and a brief description, and we will consider adding it to our database.

How can I provide feedback or report an issue?

If you encounter any issues or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us using the support email provided above. We continuously enhance the platform based on user feedback.

Can I add books to my list?

Currently, Readorithm does not support personal book lists. However, this feature is planned for future updates.
